Food Delivery App Lifecycle

Start

Begin the process of ordering food through the mobile application.

➡

Open Food App

Launch the food delivery app on your smartphone.

➡

Select Restaurant

Browse and choose your preferred restaurant from the list.

➡

Add to Cart

Select food items and add them to your cart for ordering.

➡

Place Order

Confirm your selected items and complete the payment process.

➡

Track Delivery

Monitor the delivery process in real-time until arrival.

➡

Receive Order

Collect your food from the delivery partner.

➡

End

The process is complete — enjoy your meal!
